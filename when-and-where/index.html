SEEKERS CHURCH


The ceremony, reception, and dinner will be held at Seekers Church, located at 276 Carroll St NW, Washington, DC 20012. That's one block from the Takoma Metro Station, just inside the DC line. 

Plenty of parking is available on Carroll St and Eastern Ave immediately surrounding Seekers Church.

Help us get dirty


Help us get dirty


As a symbolic reminder that marriage requires nurturing and a good foundation, we'll be potting a plant as part of our ceremony.

However, we cannot do it alone. It would be appreciated and meaningful if each of you, our loved ones, brought a handful of actual, real dirt to contribute to our plant. The dirt will be a physical manifestation of the foundation and community from which our love grows. We'd especially love it if the dirt comes from somewhere special to you. 

We will have a container for you to add your contributions to when you arrive (and wipes so you don't have to worry about any mess!).
